Why Video Games?

My origin story into video games starts Command and Conquer:Zero Hour (RTS). I can remember my little brother and I both crouched around the computer screen trying to beat each campgain mission on harder and harder difficulties. Then we would talk strategy and how if you used this unit instead of that one maybe we could win. We were so into it that my dad still makes fun of us about it to this day. My goal as a game developer is to create those moments of struggle, intense stratgeizing, raging, and finally joy for others.
